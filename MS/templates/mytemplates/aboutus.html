About Saree & Stitch

#} {#
#} {#

Saree & Stitch is a Nepali fashion brand blending tradition with modern elegance. As a multi-vendor platform, we offer beautifully crafted sarees, stitched wear, and even traditional clothing for children. With bidding and thrift features, we make heritage fashion inclusive, interactive, and affordable for all.

Our Mission

#} {#
#} {# #} {#

At Saree & Stitch we’re building a vibrant, multi-vendor platform that celebrates culture, empowers local creators, and supports sustainable fashion. From sarees and stitched wear to children’s outfits, bidding, and thrift we make tradition modern, inclusive, and accessible.#} {##} {#

Our Story

#} {#
#} {#

Saree & Stitch was founded to blend timeless tradition with modern style. What began as a small vision has grown into a multi-vendor platform where local creators showcase sarees, stitched wear, and even traditional kids’ clothing. With bidding and thrift features, we’re making fashion more sustainable, inclusive, and rooted in culture — one stitch at a time.
